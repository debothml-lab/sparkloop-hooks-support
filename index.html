Support & Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

SparkLoop Hooks helps creators generate hook ideas locally on their device.

Support

If you need help with SparkLoop Hooks, contact support at mattdeboth@hotmail.com.

Privacy Policy

SparkLoop Hooks does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

The app generates hooks locally on your device. It does not require sign-in and does not transmit generated hooks to our servers.

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. Apple may process purchase-related information according to Apple's own privacy practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, contact information, location data, user-generated content, or device data from this app.

Changes

If the app's data practices change, this policy will be updated before those changes are released.
